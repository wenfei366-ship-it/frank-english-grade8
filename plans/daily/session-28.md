## ④ 实际场景口语（20分钟）

**场景：在美国看医生，描述哪里不舒服（就医）**
（Frank 说话占比 ≥70%，用上 bleed / uncomfortable / terrible / scared + 过去进行时讲受伤经过）

设定：Frank 打篮球/骑车摔伤了，到诊所（urgent care）看医生。AI 扮演医生：
- 医生：What brings you in today? / What seems to be the problem?
- Frank 描述症状：My knee hurts. It was bleeding a lot. / I feel uncomfortable when I walk. / The pain is terrible at night.
- 医生问**经过**（正好用过去进行时）：How did it happen? → I was playing basketball when I fell down. / I was riding my bike when a car suddenly appeared.
- 医生问病史+给医嘱，Frank 听懂并确认：Should I put ice on it? / How many times a day should I take the medicine? / Do I need to come back?
- 收尾寒暄：Thank you, doctor. Have a nice day.

跑 2 轮：第 1 轮膝盖受伤；第 2 轮换症状（头疼发烧/肚子不舒服），Frank 独立完成。
