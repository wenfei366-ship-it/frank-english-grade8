# 口语任务包（按 Unit：10问答 + 5情景对话 + 3个一分钟表达 + 10造句）

# Unit 1 This is me — 口语练习包

> 用法：把某一部分发给 GPT，让它当英语老师/对话伙伴，一问一答地练。参考答案只是示范，孩子可以说自己的版本。

## 一、10个简单问答（Q&A）

1. **Q: Can you introduce yourself in three sentences?**
   A: Sure. My name is …. I'm 14 years old and I'm in Grade 8. I like playing basketball and reading.
2. **Q: What do you think of your looks?**
   A: I think I'm OK. I have short hair and I wear glasses. I have learnt to accept myself.
3. **Q: What do other people think of you?**
   A: My friends think I'm funny and kind. My mum thinks I'm a little lazy in the morning!
4. **Q: Is there anything you don't like about yourself?**
   A: I suppose so. I wasn't happy with my height before, but now I think it's not a big deal.
5. **Q: What are you good at?**
   A: I'm good at maths and drawing. I can also cook simple noodles.
6. **Q: What do you like and dislike?**
   A: I like playing football and eating strawberries. I dislike crowded buses and getting up early.
7. **Q: Have you ever changed your opinion about yourself?**
   A: Yes. I used to think I was shy, but I have already made a speech in class this year!
8. **Q: What have you learnt from Sally's story "A red, red rose"?**
   A: I have learnt that I am what I am. Everyone has something special and different.
9. **Q: Who do you want to learn from, and why?**
   A: I want to learn from Su Bingtian, because he fought his fears and never let his age hold him back.
10. **Q: What will you do to challenge yourself this term?**
    A: I will read one English book every month and stick to it. Nothing is impossible as long as I try.

## 二、5个情景对话（场景＋对话骨架）

**情景1｜新同学自我介绍**
场景（中文）：开学第一天，你旁边坐了一位新同学，你们互相认识一下。
骨架：
- A: Hi! I'm …. You're new here, right?
- B: Yes, I'm …. Nice to meet you!
- A: What do you like doing after school?
- B: I like …. What about you?
- A: I like …. We can … together sometime!

**情景2｜安慰为外貌烦恼的朋友**
场景（中文）：你的朋友因为自己戴牙套/个子矮/脸上有雀斑而不开心，你来安慰他/她（用上课文里的想法）。
骨架：
- A: I'm not happy with my …. People always look at me.
- B: I don't think it's a problem. It makes you special and different!
- A: Really? But I feel nervous about it.
- B: You know, Sally in our book hated her birthmark, but now she thinks it's like a red rose. I am what I am, right?
- A: Thank you. I feel much better now.

**情景3｜向同学推荐一本书**
场景（中文）：结对活动，向同桌介绍一本关于"发现新的自己"的书（可以用《绿野仙踪》）。
骨架：
- A: What books do you know about finding a "new me"?
- B: The Wonderful Wizard of Oz is one such book, by L. Frank Baum.
- A: Can you tell me more about it?
- B: Sure. It tells the story of Dorothy. A strong wind takes her to the Land of Oz… The most interesting part is ….
- A: What's the main idea?
- B: We already have the qualities. We just need to discover them.

**情景4｜采访一位"运动明星"**
场景（中文）：角色扮演——A 是小记者，B 扮演苏炳添（或其他运动员），做一个小采访。
骨架：
- A: Thank you for talking with us! When did you begin your sporting life?
- B: I began in …. At that time, ….
- A: What was your biggest challenge?
- B: In 2014, I changed my starting leg. It was very risky and I had to start from zero.
- A: What do you want to say to young people?
- B: Never let your age hold you back. Have a try and try harder!

**情景5｜赛前互相打气**
场景（中文）：明天要参加英语演讲比赛（或运动会），你很紧张，同学给你鼓劲。
骨架：
- A: I'm so nervous about tomorrow. What if I fail?
- B: Don't let doubts and fears hold you back!
- A: But I have never done this before.
- B: You have practised a lot. Nothing is impossible as long as you try and stick to it.
- A: You're right. I'll do my best!

## 三、3个一分钟口语表达题

1. **This is me.（这就是我）**
   提纲：开头（I'd like to share with you my true self.）→ 外貌一两句 → 喜好一两句 → 性格/能力一两句 → 结尾（I have learnt that I am what I am.）
2. **A person I admire.（我敬佩的一个人）**
   提纲：这个人是谁 → 他/她遇到过什么困难（doubts or fears）→ 他/她怎么克服（fought his/her fears, started from zero, stuck to it…）→ 取得了什么成就 → 我从中学到什么。
3. **How I accepted something about myself.（我如何接纳了自己的一点不完美）**
   提纲：以前不喜欢自己哪里（I was not happy with my…）→ 当时感觉如何（It felt just like…）→ 什么让我改变了看法（… changed me）→ 现在怎么看（I have already learnt that…）。

## 四、10个单词造句任务

（做法：孩子先用中文情境自己造英文句，再看参考句对照。）

1. **suppose** — 情境：猜测大家都有不喜欢自己的地方。
   参考：I suppose everyone has something they don't like about themselves.
2. **crowded** — 情境：描述早高峰的公交车。
   参考：The bus is always crowded at 7:30, so I go to school by bike.
3. **therefore** — 情境：因为下雨，所以体育课改在室内。
   参考：It rained hard; therefore, we had our PE class inside.
4. **although** — 情境：虽然很累，还是坚持跑完了1000米。
   参考：Although I was very tired, I finished the 1,000-metre run.
5. **already**（＋现在完成时） — 情境：告诉妈妈作业已经写完了。
   参考：Mum, I have already finished my homework!
6. **yet**（＋现在完成时否定句） — 情境：还没看那部新电影。
   参考：I haven't seen the new film yet. Let's go this weekend!
7. **doubt** — 情境：怀疑自己能不能学好游泳，但决定试试。
   参考：I doubted myself at first, but I decided to have a try.
8. **succeed** — 情境：只要坚持练习就会成功。
   参考：If you practise every day, you will succeed.
9. **satisfied** — 情境：对这次考试成绩满意/不满意。
   参考：I'm not satisfied with my score, so I will work harder.
10. **stick to** — 情境：坚持每天背10个单词的计划。
    参考：I have made a plan to learn ten words a day, and I will stick to it.

# Unit 2 Getting along — 口语训练材料

## 一、10个简单问答（先自己答，再看参考答案）

1. **Q: Do you get along well with your classmates?**
   A: Yes, I do. We study together and help each other. Sometimes we argue, but we always make up quickly.
2. **Q: How long have you known your best friend?**
   A: I have known him/her since Grade 3. We have been friends for more than five years.
3. **Q: What do you and your friends often share?**
   A: We often share books, snacks and funny stories. Sharing makes us feel closer.
4. **Q: Why is the Giant's garden always in winter?**
   A: Because the Giant is selfish. He builds a high wall to keep the children out, so spring doesn't come to his garden.
5. **Q: How does the Giant change at the end of the story?**
   A: He knocks down the wall and says, "It's your garden now." He learns to share with the children.
6. **Q: What is Liuchixiang and where is it?**
   A: It's a famous narrow lane in Tongcheng, Anhui. It's six chi wide — that's about two metres.
7. **Q: What does "Shared joy is a double joy" mean to you?**
   A: When I share my happiness with others, I feel even happier. Happiness grows when we share it.
8. **Q: What should you say when your friend feels sad?**
   A: I can say "I'm sorry to hear that" or "That sounds difficult", and then ask how I can help.
9. **Q: Is it OK to say "no" to your friends? Why?**
   A: Yes, it is. We can't always hide our feelings to please others. True friends will respect your feelings.
10. **Q: What have you learnt about getting along with others in this unit?**
    A: I've learnt to share with others, express my feelings and understand people. A little kindness encourages more kindness.

## 二、5个情景对话（两人练习）

**情景1：朋友约你坐过山车，但你恐高**
- A: The new Cloud Coaster sounds amazing! Let's go this weekend!
- B: To be honest, I'm afraid of heights. I don't really want to ride it.
- A: Oh, I'm sorry to hear that. I didn't know.
- B: I was afraid you wouldn't ask me again if I said no.
- A: Don't worry! True friends respect each other's feelings. We can play other games together.
- B: Thanks! You're a true friend.

**情景2：写作业时朋友不停发消息**
- A: You look stressed. What's wrong?
- B: I'm busy with my homework, but my best friend keeps texting me!
- A: That sounds difficult. Why not tell her the truth?
- B: What should I say?
- A: Just say, "I need to finish my homework first. Can we chat at eight?" and explain why.
- B: Good idea. She'll understand me. Thanks!

**情景3：想加入的社团和朋友们不一样**
- A: I have a problem with my friends.
- B: What is it? Tell me about it.
- A: I really want to join the dance club, but all my friends want me to join the skating club with them.
- B: I see. You can't always hide your feelings to please others.
- A: But what if they aren't happy?
- B: Don't worry. You can still spend time together after class. True friends will respect your choice.

**情景4：和同桌闹矛盾了**
- A: You look unhappy. What happened?
- B: I argued with my desk mate over a small thing, and now we don't talk.
- A: I'm sorry to hear that. Have you thought about saying sorry first?
- B: But it wasn't my fault!
- A: Remember the story of Six-chi Lane? If you step back a little, he may step back too. Kindness encourages more kindness.
- B: You're right. I'll talk to him tomorrow.

**情景5：谈谈分享的经历**
- A: Have you ever shared anything special with others?
- B: Yes! I have shared my storybooks with my cousin since last year.
- A: How does sharing make you feel?
- B: It makes me feel warm and happy. We often talk about the stories together.
- A: That's great. Sharing really brings people closer.
- B: Exactly! Shared joy is a double joy.

## 三、3个一分钟口语表达题

1. **My best friend and I**
   说一说你和好朋友认识多久了（用 We have been friends since… / for…）、你们常一起做什么、有没有闹过矛盾、怎么和好的，最后用一句话总结友谊的秘诀。
2. **A story about sharing**
   讲一次你分享东西/快乐的经历：When did it happen? What happened? How did you feel? What have you learnt from it?（至少用一次现在完成时，如 Sharing has taught me…）
3. **What I learnt from the Six-chi Lane story**
   用自己的话简单复述六尺巷的故事（two families argued over the dividing line → a poem → each stepped back three chi），再说说这个故事对你与人相处有什么启发。

## 四、10个单词造句任务（先口头造句，再写下来）

1. **since** — 用"since + 时间点"说一句你坚持了很久的事。（例：I have played the piano since 2020.）
2. **argue** — 说一句你和某人为某事争论的句子。（例：We sometimes argue over which team is better.）
3. **respect** — 用 respect 说一句关于朋友之间该怎么做的句子。（例：Good friends respect each other's feelings.）
4. **share** — 用现在完成时说一句你分享过的东西。（例：I have shared my new game with my brother.）
5. **afraid** — 说一句你害怕的东西，并加一句怎么面对它。（例：I'm afraid of the dark, but I'm getting braver.）
6. **neighbour** — 说一句关于你邻居的句子。（例：Our neighbour often helps us carry heavy bags.）
7. **reach agreement** — 说一句你们经过讨论达成一致的句子。（例：After class, we finally reached agreement on the class trip.）
8. **knock down** — 用 knock down 造一个句子。（例：Workers knocked down the old wall near our school.）
9. **whenever** — 用 whenever 说一句你的小习惯。（例：Whenever I feel tired, I listen to music.）
10. **glad** — 用 I'm so glad that… 说一句让你开心的事。（例：I'm so glad that my friend came to see me when I was ill.）

# Unit 3 Make it happen! — 口语练习

> 主题：发明创造、发明家品质、好奇心。语法重点：动词不定式和动名词作宾语（want/decide/hope + to do；enjoy/keep/practise + doing）。

## 一、10个简单问答（先自己答，再看参考答案）

1. **What great invention do you use every day?**
   参考：I use my smartphone every day. It helps me learn English and talk to my friends.
2. **Who invented the lightning rod?**
   参考：Benjamin Franklin did. He was curious about electricity and did lots of research.
3. **What did Easton build at the age of 14?**
   参考：He built a robotic arm out of plastic blocks and fishing line.
4. **Why was Jenny's artificial arm a problem?**
   参考：It cost $80,000, and children like her grow quickly, so they need new arms all the time.
5. **What do you enjoy doing after school?**
   参考：I enjoy playing basketball and reading comic books after school.
6. **What do you hope to invent one day?**
   参考：I hope to invent a robot that can clean my room and do the dishes.
7. **What are you most curious about?**
   参考：I'm most curious about space. I want to know if there is life on other planets.
8. **What qualities does a good inventor need?**
   参考：A good inventor thinks widely, never stops trying, and sometimes needs a little luck.
9. **How was the Post-it note invented?**
   参考：Spencer Silver invented a weak glue. Later, Arthur Fry found it perfect for sticking notes to paper.
10. **What can you learn from Wan Hu?**
    参考：I can learn to be brave. He was so curious about space that he tested his "flying chair" himself. Despite the failure, his curiosity gave future explorers ideas.

## 二、5个情景对话（两人练习，可换角色）

### 对话1：科学展上（介绍自己的发明）
- A: Wow, what's this? It looks cool!
- B: It's my new invention—a smart pencil box. It can warn you when you forget your homework.
- A: That blew me away! How does it work?
- B: It senses the books in your bag. Once it senses the homework is missing, it starts to beep.
- A: What a helping hand! I want to get one for myself!

### 对话2：谈论一项伟大发明（仿 Communication bank 气泡膜对话）
- A: Have you heard of bubble wrap?
- B: Sure. It's a great invention. It was invented by Alfred Fielding and Marc Chavannes in 1957.
- A: Can you tell me more about it?
- B: At first it was invented as a type of wallpaper, but it failed. The inventors didn't give up—they found more than 400 other uses!
- A: So how did it become famous?
- B: They decided to use it as packaging material. Then a computer company used it to ship computers safely, and it was a big success.

### 对话3：邀请朋友看发明展
- A: There's an "Unexpected Inventions" exhibition at the City Museum. Do you want to go?
- B: Sounds interesting! When is it?
- A: It starts on 1 May, from 9 am to 5 pm. Tickets for students are cheaper.
- B: Great. Anything special there?
- A: Yes! There's a talk by a famous inventor. We can learn about the qualities of a successful inventor.
- B: Perfect. I enjoy learning about inventions. Let's go together!

### 对话4：好奇心话题（结对讨论）
- A: Do you think inventors could work without curiosity?
- B: No way. Curiosity is the key. It drives people towards their goals.
- A: Can you give me an example?
- B: Sure. Zhang Heng was curious about how everything in the world moves. He kept studying the sky and the earth for years, and finally invented the first earthquake detector.
- A: You're right. I hope to stay curious like him!

### 对话5：帮助他人的发明（角色：记者采访小发明家）
- A: Congratulations on your award! Why did you decide to invent this smart walking stick?
- B: Thank you! I noticed that blind students had a hard time moving around. I wanted to lend them a hand.
- A: How does it work?
- B: It can sense objects nearby and shake to warn the user.
- A: What's your next plan?
- B: I hope to apply it to real life and help more blind people. I'll keep improving it!

## 四、10个单词造句任务（用指定词各造一句，最好用上 to do / doing 结构）

# Unit 4 Digital life —— 口语练习

## 一、10个简单问答（英文Q + 参考A）

1. **Q: Do you have a smartphone? What do you use it for?**
   A: Yes, I do. I use it to chat with friends, watch videos and study English on learning apps.

2. **Q: How does your family usually pay for things?**
   A: We usually use mobile payment. We just scan the QR code with our smartphones. It's fast and convenient.

3. **Q: Have you ever ridden a shared bike? How was it?**
   A: Yes, I ride shared bikes to school sometimes. They're cheap and easy to find, and they save me a lot of time.

4. **Q: What problems did the writer of "Digital shock" have in the UK?**
   A: It took her forever to find a shared bike, the shop couldn't take digital payment, and her food delivery was slow and stone-cold.

5. **Q: Why does the writer never carry her wallet in China?**
   A: Because she always makes digital payments there. She can pay for everything with her smartphone.

6. **Q: What does Timmy's mum ask him to do?**
   A: She asks him to pass her his phone, because he has been glued to it for hours. She even sends him to Grandpa's house.

7. **Q: What did Timmy do at Grandpa's house?**
   A: He went fishing and camping with Grandpa. They caught a fish and cooked it on the campfire. He had lots of fun.

8. **Q: Who teaches Margie in "The Fun They Had"?**
   A: A machine teaches her. Her classroom is right next to her bedroom, and the machine gives her lessons on a screen.

9. **Q: What is one advantage of online learning apps?**
   A: They help us learn new things anytime, anywhere. They can also let us check our daily progress.

10. **Q: Do you think digital life is good or bad for us?**
    A: I think it's mostly good because it makes life convenient. But we shouldn't let digital devices take up all our time.

## 二、5个情景对话

## 三、3个一分钟口语表达题

1. **My digital life（我的数字生活）**
   提示：用一分钟介绍你一天中用到的数字技术——早上用手机闹钟起床？用学习 app 背单词？家人用手机支付买菜？周末看在线视频？至少用到 3 个单元词汇（如 app, convenient, payment, save, advantage），并用一句 "Digital technology helps me (to)… / lets me…" 的宾补句型。

2. **Should parents limit children's screen time?（父母该不该限制孩子的屏幕时间？）**
   提示：先亮观点（I think… / In my opinion…），给出两条理由（健康 health、学习 learning），举一个自己或 Timmy 的例子（be glued to the phone → neck pain），最后总结（Smartphones are useful, but we shouldn't let them take up all our time.）。

3. **Life in 50 years' time（50年后的生活）**
   提示：想象50年后的数字生活——学校会是什么样？购物、看病、交通会有多数字化？用 "I believe that… / Maybe… will… / Technology will allow us to…" 等句型，最后说说这种变化是好是坏。

## 四、10个单词造句任务

> 要求：用下列单词各造一个自己的句子（先口头说，再写下来）。括号内为提示。

1. **convenient**（方便的）——提示：谈谈某样数字技术为什么方便。
   例：Mobile payment is very convenient because I don't need to bring my wallet.
2. **payment**（支付）——提示：搭配 make digital payments。
   例：My parents make digital payments almost every day.
3. **app**（应用程序）——提示：说一个你常用的 app 及用途。
   例:I use a learning app to practise English every morning.
4. **forever**（很长时间）——提示：搭配 It took forever to…
   例：It took forever to download the game last night!
5. **except**（除……之外）——提示：搭配 except for。
   例：Except for maths, I have finished all my homework.
6. **advantage**（优点）——提示：One advantage of… is…
   例：One advantage of shared bikes is that they are cheap.
7. **effect**（影响）——提示：have a good/bad effect on…
   例：Playing on the phone too long has a bad effect on my eyes.
8. **save**（节省）——提示：save time/money。
   例：Ordering food online saves my mum a lot of time.
9. **strange**（奇怪的）——提示：It is/was strange to do sth。
   例：It was strange to see my grandpa using a smartphone to pay.
10. **progress**（进步）——提示：make progress / check my progress。
    例：The app lets me check my progress, and I'm making great progress in English.

# Unit 5 Play by the rules — 口语练习

## 一、简单问答（10个）

1. **Q: What rules do you follow at home?**
   参考A: I have to finish my homework before playing, and I go to bed before ten. These rules help me keep a good habit.
2. **Q: What rules should we follow on the train?**
   参考A: We should be quiet, avoid smelly food and look after our children. Rules are there for us to follow.
3. **Q: Why did the writer cover his ears on the train?**
   参考A: He covered his ears in order to block the noise coming from the girl's loud music.
4. **Q: How did the writer feel when the boy kicked his seat?**
   参考A: His anger grew and his face turned as red as a tomato.
5. **Q: Do you always follow rules? Give an example.**
   参考A: Yes, mostly. For example, I always wear a seat belt in the car so as to stay safe on the road.
6. **Q: Is it ever OK to break rules?**
   参考A: In my opinion, we should not break rules. But in some urgent situations, like rushing a sick person to hospital, it might be okay.
7. **Q: What is a strong password like?**
   参考A: It should be strong and long. Don't use words that are easy to guess, like your birthday, and change it often.
8. **Q: Why shouldn't we post personal information online?**
   参考A: Because the internet has risks. Strangers may cheat us, so only people we know should see our information.
9. **Q: What is cyberbullying?**

10. **Q: What new rule does your class need?**
    参考A: I think we need the rule "Please keep our classroom clean." It brings a better learning place to us.

## 二、情景对话（5个）

### 对话1：火车上的不速之客（复述课文情境）

## 三、一分钟口语表达题（3个）

1. **A rule I follow every day**
   提示：说一条你每天遵守的规则（交通/学校/家庭均可）。结构：这条规则是什么 → 你怎么做（用 to / in order to / so as to 说目的）→ 为什么它有用（This rule is useful because…）→ 你学到了什么。
2. **Should we ever break rules?**
   提示：先亮出你的观点（In my opinion, I believe that…）→ 举一个例子支撑（比如课本中出租车司机送急病乘客的故事）→ 用短句总结（In conclusion, for one thing, …; for another, …）。
3. **How to stay safe online**
   提示：从课文5条规则中挑3条展开：keep personal information private / be password smart / be aware of strangers / be careful with your posts / ask for help。每条说"该做什么 + 为什么"。

## 四、单词造句任务（10个）

用下面的单词各造一个句子（提示可参考，不要照抄课文原句）：

1. **reduce** —— 提示：说说你想减少什么（噪音/垃圾/看屏幕的时间）。
2. **passenger** —— 提示：描述公交车或火车上的乘客。
3. **sign** —— 提示：你在学校或公园见过什么标志牌？它写着什么？
4. **urgent** —— 提示：什么情况算紧急情况？遇到时该怎么办？
5. **avoid** —— 提示：avoid doing sth，说一件你尽量避免做的事。

# Unit 6 When disaster strikes — 口语练习

> 主题：自然灾害与防灾自救 | 核心语法：过去进行时（was/were doing；when/while）

## 一、10个简单问答（英文Q + 参考A）

1. **Q: What natural disasters do you know?**
   A: I know earthquakes, floods, tsunamis, wildfires and hurricanes.
2. **Q: What were you doing at eight o'clock last night?**
   A: I was watching TV with my family at eight o'clock last night.
3. **Q: What is Pompeii?**
   A: It's a lost city in Italy. Mount Vesuvius erupted in the year 79 and ash covered the whole city.
4. **Q: What was Pliny the Younger doing when the ground shook?**
   A: He was sleeping when the ground shook very hard.
5. **Q: How old was Tilly Smith when she saved people from the tsunami?**
   A: She was only ten years old.
6. **Q: How did Tilly Smith know the tsunami was coming?**
   A: She saw the water going away from the beach. She had learnt about tsunamis at school two weeks earlier.
7. **Q: What can a disaster warning app do?**
   A: It can warn you about upcoming disasters, tell you how serious they might be, and show you the safest place to go.
8. **Q: What should you do during an earthquake?**
   A: I should stay calm and hide under a strong table. I shouldn't use the lift.
9. **Q: Who is the "Iron girl"?**
   A: She is Niu Yu. She lost her right leg in the 2008 Wenchuan earthquake, but she bravely came out of the shadows.
10. **Q: How can we help disaster survivors?**
    A: We can listen to them, encourage them to look forward, and give them support like family and friends do.

## 二、5个情景对话

### 对话1：昨晚停电（练 was/were doing + when）
A: You didn't answer my phone call last night. What were you doing?
B: Sorry! I was taking a shower when you called. Then the lights suddenly went out!
A: Really? What did you do?
B: My dad found a torch, and we were playing cards when the lights came back on.

### 对话2：谈论一次台风（灾害经历）
A: There was a big typhoon in our city last summer. Do you remember?
B: Of course! The rain was terrible. Many trees fell down and some homes were broken.
A: What were you doing when it hit our city?
B: We were staying at home. My mum was cooking and I was watching the news about the typhoon.
A: I'm glad everyone was safe. We should always listen to the weather warnings.

### 对话3：介绍防灾App（练"介绍产品"）
A: What's that on your phone?
B: It's a safety app called SAFETY NET. It shows you a map, and you can check your area.
A: How does it help in disasters?
B: It warns you about upcoming disasters like earthquakes, wildfires and tsunamis, and tells you the safest place to go.
A: That sounds useful! How much does it cost?
B: It's free! But the warning time for earthquakes is very short, so you have to act quickly.

### 对话4：地震演练（防灾自救建议）
A: We're going to have an earthquake drill tomorrow. Do you know what to do?
B: Yes. When the alarm goes off, we should hide under our desks first.
A: Right. And then?
B: When the shaking stops, we should leave the classroom quickly and go to the playground. Remember, don't use the lift and don't push others.
A: Great. It's important to stay calm in a real earthquake.

### 对话5：谈论"钢铁女孩"牛钰（灾后恢复+情感表达）
A: I read a story called "Iron girl" today. It really moved me.
B: What is it about?
A: A girl called Niu Yu. She lost her right leg in the Wenchuan earthquake when she was only 11.
B: That's so sad. How is she now?
A: She came out of the shadows with the support of her family and friends. She even finished a half marathon and carried the torch at the World University Games!
B: Wow, she's really brave. Like the writer said, "Be the heroine of your life, not the victim."
