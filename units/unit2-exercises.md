## Understanding ideas（课本p20–24）课文：The Selfish Giant

**练习1（读前预测）**
看标题和插图，用所给词预测课文内容：garden / selfish / play / children / angry / spring

**练习2（读中理解）**
Read the passage. What does the Giant do to his garden?

**练习3（选寓意）**
Choose the message of the passage:
a. Never make friends with selfish people.
b. Anger leads to unhappiness.
c. Shared joy is a double joy.

**练习4（情节图填空，用课文词汇短语补全）**
- Beginning: The Giant has a large ¹______ and the children ²______ in it every afternoon.
- Rising action: He shouts at them in a very ³______ voice. … Since then, ⁴______ has not come to his garden.
- Climax: He jumps out of bed and ⁵______. … He thinks he has been ⁶______.
- Ending: The Giant ⁷______ the wall and tells ⁸______ the garden is theirs.

**Think and share（课本p22）**
1. Why doesn't spring come to the Giant's garden?
2. Why do you think the Giant changes his mind?
3. Do you know a story with a similar message, either from a book or from your own life?（Learning to think：比较不同故事再形成自己的理解）

**练习5（语法归纳）**
读句子并总结语法规则，再从课文中找出更多同结构句子：
(a) The Giant has been away **for** a long time.
(b) **Since** then, he hasn't seen a child there.

3. ______ last year, we've shared a lot of happy moments.

**练习7（新闻短文填空，8空：填 for、since 或括号内动词的正确形式）**

**练习8（结对口语：分享经历）**

**练习2（再听，补全想法+选情绪 😢/😀，5空）**

## Developing ideas 读写（课本p28–31）课文：Six chi away, but closer together

**练习1（读前看图答问题，用词框词汇）**
词框：reach agreement / argue over / dividing line / neighbour / give up / kindness
1. What can you see in the picture?
2. What do you think the story is about?

**练习2（读文验证）**
Read the story and check your answers.

**练习3（按结构答8题）**
- Introduction: 1. Where is Liuchixiang in the story? 2. What's special about it?
- The problem: 3. What did the two families argue about? 4. Who did the Zhang family ask for advice?
- The solution: 5. What suggestion did the Zhang family receive? 6. What did the Zhang family do then? 7. How did the Wu family behave?

**Think and share（课本p30）**

2. Why does the writer use the poem in this story?（Learning to think：作者引用他人的话可以让观点更有力、更可信）
3. What have you learnt from the story?

**练习4（诗歌赏析，3题）**
1. How do you understand "as a gift" and "as time continues to drift" in the poem?
2. What rhymes can you find in the poem?
3. What's the effect of these rhymes?

**练习5（写作：写一篇关于"人与人之间互相理解"的小故事）**
- Step 1 组织思路，回答6个问题：When did the story happen? / Where did the story happen? / Who were in the story? / What problem did they face? / How did they solve the problem? / What does the story teach us about relationships?
- Step 2 借助图表写故事。
- Step 3 自查：是否包含人物、场景和事件？是否使用了课文中的词汇和表达？是否给出了关于理解他人重要性的看法？
- Step 4 与全班分享。

## Presenting ideas（课本p32–33）

**任务：Create a guide for getting along with others（制作《与人相处指南》）**
- Step 1 小组列出相处中的常见问题和可能的解决办法，投票选定一个问题。
- Step 2 按提纲写指南：
  - Introduction（说明与人相处的重要性）：Getting along with others is important because… / You will always live / study / work with others, so…
  - Body（描述一个常见问题）：A common problem is… / Sometimes you may argue over… / Perhaps you can't reach agreement on… / I can't understand why… / …has been… for a long time.
  - Body（解释解决办法）：The most important thing is… / When having an argument about…, we need to… / If you disagree about…, you should… / We could choose to step back and…
  - Conclusion（好的关系如何改变生活）：Better relationships will help you… / It's good to see how kindness encourages more kindness.
- Step 3 练习并向全班展示。
- Step 4 评选最佳三份指南和最佳三个展示。
