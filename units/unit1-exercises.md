**练习7｜短文填空（现在完成时，4空）**

## Presenting ideas（第16–17页）

**任务｜Make a speech about yourself（4步）**
- Step 1: 回看 Starting out 的思维导图，思考对自己的认识是否改变。考虑：looks / likes and dislikes / personality / abilities。
- Step 2: 按提纲组织演讲：
  - Introduction（说明目的/主旨）：My understanding of myself has changed… / I'd like to share with you my true self.
  - Body（描述外貌、喜好、性格、能力等）：I have got… / I used to…, but now I can… / I like… about myself. / I've… for the first time.＋（解释变化原因）One reason for… is… / I've learnt to accept…
  - Conclusion（以自我评价收尾）：I have learnt that I am what I am. / I have discovered… / Understanding myself means…
- Step 3: Practise and give your speech to the class.
- Step 4: Vote on the best three speeches.
