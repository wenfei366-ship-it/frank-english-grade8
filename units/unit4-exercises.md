## Presenting ideas（p64）辩论：Is digital life good or bad for us?

- **Step 1** 分组选立场；从以下角度搜集数字生活的利与弊：communication / learning / music & movies / safety / health / news
- **Step 2** 按提纲组织观点：
  - Opening 亮明立场：We believe that… / In our opinion, …
  - 多轮交锋：
    - 论点（支持/反对）：Digital technology lets people live a more convenient life. / … is really useful, but we shouldn't let it take up all our time. / … allows us to… / It has made life easier / harder by…
    - 用例子或亲身经历支撑：For example, … / … is a good example of this.
    - 反驳对方：I'm afraid I have to disagree. / Team A / B has said… But in fact, …
  - Conclusion 总结本队要点：There is no doubt that… / As we have discussed, … / It's clear that we should / shouldn't…
- **Step 3** 排练并在班里进行辩论。
- **Step 4** 全班投票评最佳队伍，评价标准：listening carefully / not interrupting / using kind words / speaking clearly
