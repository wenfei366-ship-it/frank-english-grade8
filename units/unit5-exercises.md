### Grammar（课本第71–72页，Grammar rules ⇢ 课本第131页）

**练习5｜归纳语法规则**
读课文原句，总结语法规则（动词不定式作目的状语），再从课文中找出更多同结构句子：
- (a) I got on the train **to** go home.
- (b) I went to a quiet area **in order to** read.
- (c) I covered my nose **so as to** reduce the smell coming from Mr Smelly Food.

**练习6｜用 to / in order to / so as to 合并改写句子（3题）**
1. People wear seat belts in cars. People want to stay safe on the road.
2. Sally arrived at the train station before 6:00 am. She needed to catch the earliest train.
3. I listened carefully in maths class. I hope to improve my grades.

**练习7｜用括号内提示补全书信（5空，考查目的状语）**

His honesty made me respect him. Telling the truth isn't always easy. However, he followed the rules in his heart.
I'm 5____ (sharing my story, spread some happiness). In the story, we can see the kindness in small things.

**练习8｜两人结对讲"守规则的故事"（口语）**

可用句式：Last…, I… / Please… / I love doing… in order to… / People have to… / I tried to… / This rule is useful because… / From this experience, I have learnt that…

**Think and share（讨论，3题）**

2. Why do people need to make new rules?

**练习5｜写作任务：为现代生活新事物写一份规则清单（5步）**
- Step 1 选对象：Online shopping / Using AI / Using smartphones / …?
- Step 2 调研并记笔记：①新事物是什么？②为什么需要为它立规则？③已有哪些规则？
- Step 3 依据笔记写规则清单
- Step 4 自查：是否涵盖 Step 2 三个要点？是否使用了课文词汇表达？是否说明了遵守理由？
- Step 5 与全班分享

## 五、Presenting ideas（课本第80–81页）

**任务：为你的班级设计新规则（4步）**
- Step 1 小组选题（classroom teamwork / breaktime behaviour / in-class behaviour），讨论：What are the problems? What rules could solve the problems?
- Step 2 制作规则板（rule board），每条规则说明 What it's about（The rule is "Please be…" / "No…" / "Please do / don't…"; The aim of this rule is to…）和 Why it's important（It brings… to us. / It tries to stop… / With this rule, there won't be… / There will be fewer / less…）
- Step 3 练习并向全班展示
- Step 4 投票选出最佳三个规则板和最佳三个展示
